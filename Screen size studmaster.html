1) Once on the StudMaster main screen click on the small C:\ button top left of StudMaster window.
2) Choose Defaults on the menu.
3) Choose the Layout tab.
4) Screen buffer size should be set to Width=80 and Height=25. Wrap text on.
5) Window size should be set to Width=80 and Height=25.
6) Choose the Font tab.
7) Choose Font=Consolas.
8) Choose Size=28 for a start. (or 24 if 28 does not fit on the screen)
9) Click OK
10) Again click on the smll C:\ button top left of StudMaster window.
11) Choose Properties on the menu.
12) Repeat steps 3 to 9 above.
13) StudMaster window should be larger on the screen.
14) The font size in step 8 could be played with and made bigger, but at some stage window would not fit screen. Then too big.
